F.7 & For Alison

Vinko Globokar's F.7 is a rhythmic combination of vocal sounds (g, p, t, d, s, f), body hits and scratches.
Pauline Oliveros' For Alison Knowles is an invitation to 'perform a sound, gesture, word, movement, graphic for each year of your life'. Each year could also be subdivided in "seasons, months, weeks, days, hours, moments".

For the last month I have been recording Globokar's F.10 (the 'gesture' mentioned in Oliveros' meditation) in different places and (at least) once a day. The various recordings were then broken down into fragments and juxtaposed to reconstruct the entire original performance, creating a complex network of spatial and temporal interactions.
As I plan to continue recording Globokar's fragment once a day for the next month (or a few months), the version presented here should be considered provisional.
